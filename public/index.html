The luxurious, stylish, bright and spacious 'Mountain View Paradise' is situated in one of the most scenic places in Idukki district. Set back from the main road behind secure electronic gates and accessed via a sweeping driveway this is an incredible home having modern amenities with superb landscaped front and rear gardens, offering extreme seclusion and bliss.

This bungalow is built on a 3-acre private plantation property. The inside living accommodation is beautifully designed. The ground floor comprises a foyer, a formal living room and a family living room both beautifully decorated with magnificent chandeliers, lampshades, paintings, curios, rare Chinese potteries etc.

There is a modular kitchen featuring a sleek culinary inspired design with various facilities like kitchen aid, oven, microwave etc for food lovers who like to cook and experiment. This place also has a second kitchen with a storage room and fitted utility with washing machine and dryer and iron. The kitchen opens into a large dining space which has access to a colourful sprawling rear lawn with orchids and exotic plants.

The first floor also comprises of a well-done bedroom with a luxurios ensuite bathroom, study with a library for those book lovers, computers and 24 hr wifi facility. The top floor consists of 2 luxurios bedroom with ensuite bathrooms, one of which has a balcony with a mountain view and a terrace space for yoga.

There's also a home theatre with wooden flooring, an indoor badminton court for those game lovers and a fully equipped multi gym.

This property suits those who like to just relax and enjoy nature, meditate etc as well as those who like to visit nearby places. Tours can be arranged to Ramakkalmedu (11 miles), Thekkady (26 miles), and Munnar (31 miles) on request.
This incredible property is truly a heaven on earth.
